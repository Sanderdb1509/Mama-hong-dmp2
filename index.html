About Us

Mama Hong is a family-owned Vietnamese food truck in Rotterdam, built on generations of culinary heritage and a passion for authentic street food. What started as a family tradition rooted in Vietnamese home cooking has grown into a trusted name for fresh, handmade Vietnamese street food enjoyed by locals and visitors across the city.

More than just a food truck, Mama Hong represents family, tradition, and community. Each visit is an invitation to experience Vietnamese culture through food – warm, honest, and made to be shared. Whether you are discovering Vietnamese street food for the first time or returning for a favourite dish, Mama Hong offers a taste of Vietnam, right in Rotterdam.

Our Story

"WHAT I MAKE FOR MYSELF, I ALSO MAKE FOR YOU. THE GUARANTEE THAT YOU WILL RECEIVE NOTHING BUT THE VERY BEST."

In 1979, Mama Hong emigrated from Vietnam to the Netherlands, bringing with her a passion for authentic Vietnamese cuisine. What started as cooking for family and neighbors quickly grew into something more. Her spring rolls and traditional dishes became so popular that she began sharing her culinary heritage with the wider community.

Today, Mama Hong personally selects every ingredient, ensuring that each dish meets her exacting standards. She takes pride in offering products that she would serve to her own family—a guarantee of quality, freshness, and authentic flavor that has made her a beloved name throughout Rotterdam.
